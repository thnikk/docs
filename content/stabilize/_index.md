If you're trying to use your keypad on a large deskmat, the tape can be ineffective since it works best on smooth surfaces.

## Laminated paper

This is an easy trick to help keep your setup portable and your keypad stable without needing to permanently fix it to something.

What you'll need:

- Paper
- Scissors
- Tape

### Paper

Cut a piece of paper down to roughly 4x12 mm. This doesn't need to be precise, but keep the width around 4 mm (1/2 in) to keep it within the tape.

### Tape

Cut a strip of tape longer than 12 mm.
